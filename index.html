About Us

Money Moves explores the rapid digitization of remittances in Latin America. With over $160.9 billion sent to the region last year, remittances play a vital role in household economies—and the high fees charged by traditional money transfer operators (MTOs) like Western Union and MoneyGram remain a critical challenge.


This project investigates how fintech companies are working to reduce these costs and reshape the remittance landscape through technology and innovation. We explore both the opportunities and the obstacles facing this transition.


Together, we aim to inform and inspire conversations about financial inclusion and the future of cross-border payments in Latin America.
